Anglo-Saxon 3x3s

Warrior

Danes attack always.
Warriors must defend.
Family comes first.

Woman

I am treasured.
Strength bedecks beauty.
I own property.

Child

Sons're like fathers.
Grow up fast; 
Be tough—survive.

Thane

Fighting serves kings:
Property is costly.
Landlord leads peasentry.

King

Lord—recieve tribute.
Ruling doesn’t last;
Fear sometimes perpetuates.
